Bisteron is a clean futurist typeface with a rounded, technical feel and a calm confidence in its shapes.

Built around soft corners and controlled geometry, it balances friendliness with precision. The letterforms feel engineered rather than drawn, giving text a sense of structure and motion without becoming cold. Its multiweight family allows the tone to shift from light and airy to solid and assertive, while the italics introduce a subtle forward lean that feels smooth rather than aggressive.

Bisteron was designed to be flexible and readable, working just as well in short statements as in longer passages. It feels at home in modern interfaces, experimental visuals, and imagined futures where technology is approachable and thoughtfully designed.

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers. 

Bisteron is a personal exploration of soft futurism, focusing on clarity, rhythm, and balance. It avoids sharp edges in favor of smooth transitions. The goal was to create a typeface that feels reliably human, even when placed in highly technical settings.
